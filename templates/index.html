"When can I retire?"

is an important question that people of all ages ask.

Retirement and financial independence can often seem like nothing more than a pipe dream for the average person. If you have an extremely low or a zero savings rate, you will likely have to work for the rest of your life just to survive.

Fortunately, the inverse is also true. The higher your savings rate, the faster you can retire - regardless of how much you make. A janitor that saves thirty-five percent of his forty-thousand a year income can retire much earlier than a CEO making one million a year but only saving fifteen percent.
